Frequently Asked Questions

What types of partnerships does Ni-Wakati Sports Africa offer?

+

We offer various partnership models including: Corporate Sponsorships (financial and in-kind), Program Partnerships (co-designing and implementing sports initiatives), NGO Collaborations (joint projects and resource sharing), Government Partnerships (policy development and national programs), and Academic Partnerships (research and student placements).

Can I volunteer remotely or do I need to be in Africa?

+

We offer both on-ground and virtual volunteering opportunities. Virtual volunteers can contribute through skills like digital marketing, graphic design, content writing, data analysis, research, grant writing, and social media management.

How are donations used and what's the impact?

+

85% of donations go directly to program implementation, 10% to organizational capacity building, and 5% to administrative costs. All donors receive annual impact reports.

What skills are needed for volunteering?

+

We need diverse skills including: Sports coaching, Event management, Community mobilization, Digital skills, Research and data analysis, Grant writing, Translation, and Professional services.

How are partnerships structured and measured?

+

Partnerships begin with a discovery phase, followed by co-creation of objectives. We establish clear KPIs including youth reached, community engagement metrics, skill development outcomes, and economic impact.
